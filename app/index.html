Game control

To move the ship to the left, press the "Arrow Left" key. To move the ship to the left in the mobile version of the game, click the button with Chevron Left.

To move the ship to the right, press the "Arrow Right" key. To move the ship to the right in the mobile version of the game, click the button with Chevron Right.

If you want to make a shot, press the up key. If you want to make a shot in the mobile version of the game, click the button with a Circle.

Good luck and enjoy the game!
